About TED

TED is a non profit organisation started in 1984 as a conference displaying innovation in Technology, Entertainment and Design. TED is an event that brings together the world’s largest thinkers and doers to share ideas that matter in any discipline. TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives like TED Conferences, TEDGlobal, TED Translators, TEDx, TED Fellow, TED ED, TEDMED, TED Books, TEDWomen, TED Salon, To know more about TED, Visit www.ted.com also follow TED on Twitter at twitter.com/TEDTalks on Facebook at facebook.com/TED, and on Instagram at Instagram.com/TED

About TEDx

TEDx are independent TED-like events to elongate the spirit of “Ideas Worth Spreading”, Where, x = Independently Organised Event. which can be organised by anyone who obtains a free license from TED. TEDx events has a sole purpose of providing a TED like experience to it's attendees. A TEDx event is organised by volunteers from the local community, and just like TED events, it lacks any commercial, religious, or political agenda. At a TEDx event, Prerecorded TED Talks and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection.

Theme

The Change

The idea on which the event is based on is something that is a basic unit of evolution, something that is certain, something that is constant, and something that is inevitable. Yes, “the change”. When we think about the word 'CHANGE' and an array of quotes stated by some magnificent personalities crosses our mind- "The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do”. -Steve Jobs There are a dozen of beautiful statements that tells us that it's our responsibility to change. Life in general is about it. There is no standstill in the world. You're either going backwards or moving forward. Going backwards isn't an option, that's axiomatic and moving forward isn't a cake walk, but ‘flex’ is the key. Change doesn't take place abruptly as Rome wasn't built in a day. Join us at TEDxGHRCE 2018 as we explore the incredible power of authentic Change — the kind that drives resilience, power, and beauty for all humankind. Stay tuned for more information and be sure to follow @TEDxGHRCE on social media as we share more about this year’s event.
